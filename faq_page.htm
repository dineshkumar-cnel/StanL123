  • I accidentally deleted the Excel spreadsheet and I can’t find my Zip file. Can I download another copy?

          No. All sales are final. After you download the Zip file, it is highly recommended that you backup the Zip file on another media for safekeeping.

  • What do I have to do in order to win playing Game of Trends?

          You start by trading U.S. stocks only for 10-months. You conclude with a season ending winning trade performance of  90% or better.

  • How many unsuccessful attempts do I have before I can no longer qualify as a winner playing Game of Trends?

 Given the fact that Game of Trends is played on an annual basis, the younger you are, the more attempts you have to win the game. For example, assume you stop trading stocks at age 80 and you are now age 32. You would have a maximum of  48 unsuccessful attempts to achieve a 90% or better winning efficiency in trade performance at the end of any given 10-mionth trading season.

In addition during this timeframe, you would also have a maximum of 480 monthly attempts to achieve the status of Trendwin Master! [Qualifying information on attaining this achievement status can be found in Hall of Trendwin Masters!]
